Amsterdam

History Museum

The museum witnesses millions of tourists every year. Although, the museum remained closed before, these days because of the extended renovation activities, a small part of the museum remains open for the tourists. By visiting this small portion, you can imagine and visualize the rest of the museum, which makes the experience pretty interesting and enriching. Rijksmuseum is one of the best Amsterdam tourist attractions added in Amsterdam itinerary 3 days and 5 Days in Amsterdam itinerary. Must visit this awesome attraction.

Boating

The old centers of the city are surrounded and built upon the canal ring which makes the city look most unique in the world. These canals will give you a sense of freedom, peace and space which indeed produces a feel good factor for the tourists. And this uniqueness makes the place more lovable and interesting to experience. You can rent a boat, cruise through the canals and even live in a houseboat as well. The ambience gets livelier and more interesting with the flocking of birds, people belonging to different cultures, heritage buildings and multi-cuisine cum ethnic restaurants situated along the canals. Amsterdam Canals is one of the best amsterdam attractions added in Amsterdam itinerary 2 days and Amsterdam itinerary 5 Days.

Zoo

Natura Artis Magistra is a majestic zoo in Amsterdam. It is one of the oldest zoos and top tourist attraction in Amsterdam, Netherlands and also in mainland Europe. The zoo features some of the most magnificent creatures in the area and comes highly recommended by travellers all across the globe for its grandeur. Visit the zoo as it offers some of the best quality time to be spent with your family or friends. The zoo also features a top class aquarium and a planetarium.
