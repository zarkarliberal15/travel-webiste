Srinagar

Shikara Ride at Dal Lake

Ride a Shikara, the traditional wooden boat, in the evening at Dal Lake and experience the natural beauty of the glistening lake. Reach the activity venue and board the boat from the jetty. As you float across the serene lake, witness the floating market and marvel at the amazing reflection of mountains in the water. Enjoy the soothing atmosphere as the scenic view of the surrounding mountains, clear sky and the lake envelops you in its serenity. Take charming pictures against the breathtaking backdrop of the vast lake and the picturesque landscape. After an hour-long ride, you will be taken back to the jetty to continue with your evening. This experience includes a Shikara ride at Dal Lake. The duration of the activity is 1 hour.

Sonmarg

Literally meaning the Meadow of Gold, Sonmarg charms with its vast green valley flanking the gushing Indus River. When in Sonmarg, you could take a tour of the famous Thajiwas Glacier. You could either walk for three hours or take a horse ride to reach Thajiwas Glacier. At an altitude of 9,186 feet, Thajiwas Glacier is a sparkling white ice sheet set against emerald meadows. Keep your camera handy to click pictures of the hills and glaciers, which are often enclosed with fog. One could also visit the Kheer Bhawani Temple, situated in the middle of a natural spring. Consider visiting Gadsar Lake too as from here the panoramic views of the frozen Satsar and Baltan Lakes are breathtaking.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a town, a hill station, a popular skiing destination and a notified area committee in the Baramulla district of the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. The town is situated in the Pir Panjal Range in the western Himalayas.
